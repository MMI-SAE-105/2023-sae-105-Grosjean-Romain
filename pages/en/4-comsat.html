COMSAT Laboratories

(Clarksburg, Maryland - US) • 1969

Avant-Garde in Technology

My buildings are like my children, so I cannot have favorites.

Architectural Style and Influence

A true fusion of postmodern aesthetics and functionality, COMSAT Laboratories, designed by renowned architect César Antonio Pelli, embodies the successful fusion of modern aesthetics and functionality. As a research and development centre, the building's distinctive forms reflect an avant-garde approach, creating a sophisticated place at the heart of the technological landscape.

Architectural avant-garde

Pelli was renowned for his avant-garde approach to architectural design, and COMSAT Laboratories is a prime example of this. The building's distinctive forms bear witness to his commitment to innovation and his constant concern to combine architectural beauty with practical utility.

Innovation in Design

Located at the heart of Clarksburg's technological and natural landscape, COMSAT Laboratories stands out for its successful integration into this environment. The bold architectural choices reflect Pelli's desire to push back the boundaries while creating a functional and aesthetically pleasing space.

Legacy

His work is evident in projects such as COMSAT Laboratories. His ability to integrate architectural beauty into specific functional environments is testament to his enduring influence on contemporary architecture.
